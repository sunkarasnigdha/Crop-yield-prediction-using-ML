About Us

Welcome to the Crop Yield Prediction System — a smart tool built with machine learning to help farmers and agricultural experts predict crop yields more accurately than ever before!

  • 📅 Years Covered: 1990 to 2013 historical agricultural data
  • 🌧️ Rainfall Insights: Regional average annual rainfall
  • 🌡️ Temperature Records: Annual average temperatures
  • 🧪 Pesticide Usage: Tonnes of pesticide applied per country
  • 🌍 Geographic Reach: Data from 101 countries worldwide
  • 🌽 Crop Categories: 10 major crops including grains, pulses, and roots

Our goal is to bridge data science and agriculture to empower smarter, more sustainable farming decisions 🌱.
