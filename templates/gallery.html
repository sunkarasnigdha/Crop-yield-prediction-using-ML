Crop Gallery

Wheat

Wheat: Grows well in temperate climates with moderate rainfall.
  • Ideal Climate: Temperate
  • Temp: 15–25°C
  • Rainfall: 500–1000 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Season: Rabi

Maize

Maize: Needs sunny weather and fertile soil.
  • Ideal Climate: Warm and sunny
  • Temp: 18–27°C
  • Rainfall: 500–800 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Fertile, well-drained loam
  • Season:Kharif

Paddy (Rice)

Paddy: Grows best in flooded fields with high rainfall and warm temperatures.
  • Ideal Climate: Hot and humid
  • Temp: 20–35°C
  • Rainfall: 1200–2000 mm/year
  • Soil Type:Clayey, water-retentive
  • Season:Kharif (Monsoon)

Potatoes

Potatoes: A drought-tolerant grain ideal for arid and semi-arid regions.
  • Ideal Climate: Cool, temperate
  • Temp: 15–20°C
  • Rainfall: 500–700 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Sandy loam, well-drained
  • Season:Rabi (Winter)

Sorghum

Sorghum: Thrive in cool climates with well-drained, fertile soil.
  • Ideal Climate: Semi-arid, drought-resistant
  • Temp: 25–30°C
  • Rainfall: 400–700 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Sandy loam or clay loam
  • Season:Kharif or Summer

Soybeans

Soybeans: Prefer warm climates and well-drained soil, rich in organic matter.
  • Ideal Climate: Warm and moderate
  • Temp: 20–30°C
  • Rainfall: 500–1000 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Loamy, rich in organic matter
  • Season:Kharif

Sweet Potatoes

Sweet Potatoes: Grow well in warm climates with loose, sandy soil.
  • Ideal Climate: Warm subtropical
  • Temp: 21–26°C
  • Rainfall: 750–1200 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Sandy loam, well-drained
  • Season: Kharif or early summer

Cassava

Cassava: Grows in poor soils and is drought-tolerant, ideal for tropical climates.
  • Ideal Climate: Tropical, warm
  • Temp: 25–35°C
  • Rainfall: 1000–1500 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Sandy loam, tolerates poor soil
  • Season: Year-round

Yams

Yams: Thrive in tropical regions with well-drained soil and consistent rainfall.
  • Ideal Climate: Tropical, humid
  • Temp: 25–30°C
  • Rainfall: 1000–1500 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Fertile, well-drained loam soil
  • Season: Kharif

Plantains

Plantains: Require humid tropical climates and fertile, moist soil.
  • Ideal Climate: Humid tropical
  • Temp: 26–30°C
  • Rainfall: 1200–2000 mm/year
  • Soil Type: Moist, fertile loamy soil
  • Season:Year-round
